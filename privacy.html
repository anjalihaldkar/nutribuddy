Information We Collect

We collect information only when necessary to provide you with our products and services. Here's exactly what we collect and why:

Type of Data What We Collect Why
Account Information Name, email, phone, address, password (hashed) To create your account and process orders
Child Profile Child's name, age, gender, health goals (parent-provided) To personalize supplement recommendations
Order Details Products ordered, delivery address, payment method (not card numbers) To fulfill and track your orders

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following specific purposes only:

  • Order Fulfillment: Processing purchases, arranging delivery, sending order confirmations and tracking information.
  • Personalization: Generating your child's personalized diet chart and supplement recommendations based on information you provide.
  • Customer Support: Responding to your queries, resolving issues, and improving our products based on feedback.
  • Safety & Compliance: Detecting fraudulent transactions, ensuring platform security, and complying with applicable Indian laws and regulations.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing aggregate (anonymized) usage patterns to improve our website, app, and product formulations.
  • Communications: Sending transactional emails (order updates), and, only if you've opted in, newsletters and promotional offers.
💡
We will never use your child's health data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service you specifically requested. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email or by emailing hello@nutribuddy.in.
🤝

Sharing & Disclosure

We take data sharing seriously. We only share your data when it's strictly necessary to provide our service:

  • Delivery Partners: Your name and delivery address are shared with courier partners (Delhivery, Shiprocket) to fulfill orders. They are contractually prohibited from using this data for any other purpose.
  • Payment Processors: Payment data is processed directly by our payment partners (Razorpay, Stripe). We never store your card details on our servers.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Our website and databases are hosted on AWS India (Mumbai region), which maintains SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.
  • Analytics: We use anonymized, aggregated data with Google Analytics. No personal identifiers are included. IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by a court order, law enforcement request, or other legal obligation under Indian law. We will notify you unless prohibited.
We will never: Sell your data to any third party, share your child's health information with advertisers, provide data to data brokers, or share information for any purpose beyond what is described above.

Data Security

We invest heavily in protecting your data. Our security measures include:

  • AES-256 Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest. All data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3.
  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a high cost factor. We never store plain-text passwords.
  • Payment Security: We are PCI-DSS compliant. Card processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see or store your card details.
  • Access Controls: Employee access to customer data is strictly limited on a need-to-know basis with multi-factor authentication required.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct quarterly security audits and annual penetration testing by certified third-party security firms.
  • Breach Response: In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by Indian law.
🔔
Despite our best efforts, no online system is 100% secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your NutriBuddy account and enabling two-factor authentication when available.

Children's Privacy

NutriBuddy is a service for parents of children aged 2–14. We take children's privacy with the utmost seriousness and comply fully with India's DPDP Act 2023 regarding children's data.

  • All information about children on our platform is provided by and collected from parents or legal guardians — not from children directly.
  • Children's health data (age, goals, dietary preferences) is used solely to generate personalized recommendations and diet charts for the child.
  • We never use children's data for advertising, profiling, targeting, or any commercial purpose beyond direct service delivery.
  • We do not knowingly allow children under 18 to create their own accounts or interact with our services without parental oversight.
  • Parents can request access to, correction of, or deletion of all data relating to their child at any time.
Special Protection: Children's health and dietary information receives our highest level of data protection — isolated storage, enhanced encryption, and the most restrictive access controls of any data category on our platform.
⚖️

Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights as a Data Principal:

  • Right to Information: Know what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data. We will comply within 30 days unless a legal obligation requires us to retain it.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for processing at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer (details in Section 12) or with the Data Protection Board of India.
  • Right to Nomination: Nominate another individual to exercise your rights on your behalf in case of death or incapacity.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@nutribuddy.in with the subject line "Privacy Rights Request". We will respond within 14 business days.

Third-Party Services

Our website and services integrate with the following third-party providers. Each is bound by their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy. PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Delhivery / Shiprocket: Logistics and delivery. Only your name, address, and phone number are shared.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.
